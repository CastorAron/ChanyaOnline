About the Author

Pamela Chogo is a Lecturer at the Institute of Accountancy Arusha (IAA) and the founder of Chanya Change, a social enterprise that empowers youth and women through entrepreneurship and innovation. She has over 15 years of experience as a lecturer, trainer, mentor, and career guide. She is passionate about working with young people to create an employable community. She is also an author of life-transforming books focused on youth.

Pamela Chogo is a role model for young women and girls in Tanzania. She is an inspiration to all who are working to create a more just and equitable world.
